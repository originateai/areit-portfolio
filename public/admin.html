Bootstrap Price History

One-time load of 2 years historical OHLCV data via EODHD — required for accurate strategy signals

⚠️ Run this once The strategy engine needs 200+ days of price history for RSI, moving averages, Bollinger Bands and volume ratios to be accurate. Without this, all signals are based on 1 day of data and are meaningless.
