Bootstrap Price History

One-time load of 12 months historical data — required for accurate strategy signals

⚠️ Important — run this once

The strategy engine needs at least 200 days of price history to calculate RSI, moving averages, Bollinger Bands and volume ratios accurately. Without this, signals are based on 1 day of data and are unreliable.

This function pulls 12 months of daily OHLCV from IG Markets (or Yahoo Finance as fallback) for all active stocks. Takes 30-45 minutes. Run it once then never again — daily prices keep the database current.
